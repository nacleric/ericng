Thing's I'd like to improve upon

It's always nice to have a bit of humility so that we, as software developers can recognize our flaws and improve upon them. One thing I'd like to improve on is to rely less on the power of frameworks and not take the tools I use for granted. Throughout my journey I'd like to be known as an engineer and not "that guy who only knows X framework"; because of this fear of not wanting to drop into irrelevancy. I sort of have this "obsession" with trying to use vanilla javascript so that I can prepare myself to pickup any framework without being puzzled by the "magic" it provides.This will probably make the learning process will be much more tedious but I truly believe that throwing business logic out the window and "respecting the process" is the best possible way to develop creativity and flexability.
